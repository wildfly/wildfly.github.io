The security-realm configuration is deprecated and may be removed or moved in future versions.

A group search where the group entry contains an attribute referencing it's members.

  • cache A cache for previous group to previous group-to-principal search results.
    • by-access-time A cache to hold the results of previous LDAP interactions.
    • by-search-time A cache to hold the results of previous LDAP interactions.
