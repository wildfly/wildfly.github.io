Deprecated Since 1.7.0

A simple filter configuration to identify the users distinguished name from their username.

Children (1)

  • cache A cache of previous username-filter results.
    • by-access-time A cache to hold the results of previous LDAP interactions.
    • by-search-time A cache to hold the results of previous LDAP interactions.
